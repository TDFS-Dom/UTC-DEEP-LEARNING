# DANH SACH CHU DE BAI TAP LON - MON: HOC SAU

## Yeu cau

- Moi nhom chon xay dung demo **1 mo hinh o Phan 1** va trinh bay ly thuyet ve **1 mo hinh o Phan 2**
- Lam 1 **slide** de bao cao phan BTL cua nhom
- Lam 1 **quyen bao cao** (in bia mem, <=30 trang) de bao cao ca 2 phan: 1 va 2
- Chuong trinh may tinh dung de nghiem thu **khong duoc co comment**

## Cach danh gia

| Hang muc | Diem |
|---|---|
| Quyen bao cao | 2.5 diem |
| Slide | 2.5 diem |
| Van dap rieng (hoi van dap luc trinh bay slide + van dap phan code) | 5 diem |
